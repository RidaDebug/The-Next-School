The Next School, a project of Alpha Education Network, aims to provide a multi-faceted, modern pedagogical model to the education industry of Pakistan
that will enable its students to develop independent thinking skills, the ability to distinguish between right and wrong, a positive attitude towards life and a
thirst for knowledge. The School offers an inquiry based, child centered and academically rigorous curriculum that addresses the intellectual, social and
emotional needs of each child.
Our goal is to develop young people who are open minded, caring, good communicators and ready to take responsibility for making a difference to the world.

Our Mission

To provide an innovative, diverse and efficient teaching and learning environment
which is distinctively modern and economically feasible,
and is conducive to holistic development of children across all strata of our society.

Our Vision

Delivering modern and affordable quality education to
ensure sustainable national development.

Philosophy

The mission of The Next School, in partnership with the community, is to provide
students with the skills and exploratory experiences that
enable them to reach their fullest potential as independent thinkers. We deliver a
diversified curriculum, a school environment that fosters
civic mindedness, self-esteem and respect for individual differences, and with an
innovative and technologically up to date educational
system that is affordable, we seek to address the unique needs of our dynamic society.
